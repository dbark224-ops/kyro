# Australian Legislation Knowledge Sources

This is the first-pass source collection plan for Kyro's legislation and compliance knowledge base.

The goal is to let Kyro answer Australian trade and construction questions from official public sources first, while keeping paywalled standards as references/metadata until licensing exists.

## Collection policy

Collect sources in three buckets:

1. `public_ingest`
   - Public legislation
   - Public regulations
   - Public government guidance
   - Public regulator manuals, bulletins, and practice notes
   - Public code material such as the NCC where access terms allow use

2. `metadata_only`
   - Paywalled standards
   - References to standards cited by law or guidance
   - Anything where Kyro should know the standard exists, but not store the full text yet

3. `restricted`
   - Proprietary commentary
   - Paid handbooks
   - Any source we should not ingest until rights are reviewed

## Core collection structure for every jurisdiction

For each state and territory, collect these groups:

1. Primary legislation portal
2. Building / construction regulator guidance
3. Plumbing / drainage regulator guidance where separate
4. Electrical / gas safety guidance where separate
5. WHS / construction safety guidance
6. Occupational licensing / contractor licensing material
7. Consumer-facing trade obligations, warranties, domestic building contract guidance, and dispute guidance
8. Security of payment / construction contract legislation and regulator guidance where relevant
9. Local permit, inspection, approval, and water authority guidance as a later layer

For each source, collect:

- current Act(s)
- current regulations
- public practice notes
- technical guidance
- compliance bulletins
- adoption notes / jurisdiction variations
- forms or approval guidance if it materially affects jobs
- version / effective date
- official URL

## National / federal sources

### Federal legislation
- Source: [Federal Register of Legislation](https://www.legislation.gov.au/)
- Collect:
  - Commonwealth legislation that affects trade businesses, especially consumer law, privacy, employment, and any national instruments directly relied on by trade workflows
  - Any federal instruments that feed into safety, energy, or construction compliance

### National Construction Code / ABCB
- Source: [National Construction Code](https://ncc.abcb.gov.au/)
- Collect:
  - Current NCC volumes
  - State and territory variations
  - Plumbing Code of Australia public materials
  - ABCB guidance and explanatory material
  - Adoption / transition notes between NCC editions

### Model WHS laws
- Source: [Safe Work Australia](https://www.safeworkaustralia.gov.au/)
- Collect:
  - Model WHS Act
  - Model WHS Regulations
  - Trade-relevant model codes of practice
  - Public guidance on construction, asbestos, excavation, heights, confined spaces, electrical risk, plant, manual handling, hazardous substances, and incident response

### Standards references only
- Source: [Standards Australia Store](https://store.standards.org.au/)
- Collect as metadata only for now:
  - standard number
  - title
  - issuing body
  - official purchase URL
  - where it is referenced by legislation, NCC, or regulator guidance
- Priority references to track:
  - AS/NZS 3500 series
  - AS/NZS 3000
  - gas installation and appliance standards
  - any NCC-referenced or regulator-referenced standards relevant to target trades

## Recommended document families to ingest first

If we are prioritising for Kyro rather than trying to collect everything at once, ingest in this order:

1. NCC + jurisdiction variations
2. each state's building Act and regulations
3. each state's plumbing / drainage Act and regulations
4. each state's electrical safety / gas safety legislation
5. each state's WHS legislation and construction codes of practice
6. regulator licensing guides and scope-of-work guidance
7. domestic building / consumer protection / warranties / complaint guidance
8. security of payment / construction contracts legislation

## What to leave as metadata-only for now

Do not ingest full text yet unless licensing is secured:

- AS/NZS standards
- proprietary handbooks
- paid technical commentary
- copyrighted training manuals

For now, store:
- standard number
- title
- official URL
- where it is referenced
- which trade / jurisdiction it affects
- any internal notes about why it matters
